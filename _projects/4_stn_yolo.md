Collaborating with Texas A&M AgriLife, I helped build an automated plant phenotyping system based on
a Spatial Transformer Network YOLO model. I developed a multispectral preprocessing technique using
band superimposition and min-max normalization that improved spatial resolution and signal-to-noise
ratio for downstream object detection.
