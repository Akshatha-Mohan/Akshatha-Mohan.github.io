In collaboration with the **Virtual Imaging Trials** team at Duke University (Prof. Joseph Y. Lo),
I analyze clinical and simulated CT images to study the false positives that arise in AI-based lung
cancer detection. The central question is how image quality, acquisition, and harmonization affect
downstream clinical tasks such as nodule detection and classification — connecting image-quality
behavior to task performance rather than relying on generic visual metrics alone.
