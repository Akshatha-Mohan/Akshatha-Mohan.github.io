Working with Dr. Joshua Peeples and Prof. Christie Sayes, I studied the relationship between
morphological imaging metrics — lacunarity and fractal dimension — and dendritic cell sensitization
after nanoparticle exposure. I quantified differences in cellular texture using Earth Mover's Distance
and implemented a novel **Quantization and Counting Operator (QCO)** for statistical texture analysis,
capturing spatial co-occurrence relationships in SEM images.
